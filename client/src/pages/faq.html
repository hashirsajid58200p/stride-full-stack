Shipping & Orders

Everything you need to know about delivery and order tracking.

What is your shipping policy?

We offer free standard shipping on all orders over $100. Standard shipping typically takes 5-7 business days. Express shipping options are available at checkout for faster delivery.

How do I track my order?

Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can also track your order by logging into your account and viewing your order history.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship to over 50 countries worldwide. International shipping costs and delivery times vary by location. Customs fees and duties are the responsibility of the customer.

Can I cancel or modify my order?

Orders can be cancelled or modified within 1 hour of placement. After that, the order enters our processing system and cannot be changed. Please contact customer service immediately if you need assistance.

Returns & Exchanges

Information on returning or exchanging your purchases.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 30 days of purchase. Items must be unworn, in original condition with tags attached. Refunds will be processed within 5-10 business days of receiving the returned item.

Products & Sizing

Find the perfect fit and verify authenticity.

How do I know what size to order?

Each product page includes a detailed size guide. We recommend measuring your feet and comparing them to our size chart. If you're between sizes, we suggest ordering the larger size.

Are your products authentic?

Yes, all our products are 100% authentic and sourced directly from authorized distributors. Each item comes with a certificate of authenticity.

Payments & Support

Details on billing and contacting our team.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, Apple Pay, and Google Pay. All transactions are secured with SSL encryption.

Do you offer gift wrapping?

Yes, gift wrapping is available for $5 per item. You can select this option during checkout and include a personalized message.

How can I contact customer service?

You can reach our customer service team via email at support@stride.com, by phone at 1-800-STRIDE-123, or through our live chat feature. We're available Monday-Friday, 9 AM - 6 PM EST.
